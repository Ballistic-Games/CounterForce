Game Guide

GPS-based Real-Time-Strategy

Turn your surroundings into a fortress.

Build SAM sites, defense turrets, cruise missile launchers, and nuclear missile silos to fortify your neighborhood. Form alliances and wage all-out war with enemies in the next town or the next continent. Join an active community of players and become a new friend or a new foe. Pursue peace or put your finger on the button to end the world with nuclear war!

Key Features:

  • CounterForce uses your phone's GPS location for gameplay. To build a structure in a location, you must go there.

  • Form factions with other players and compete for dominance.
  • Declare war on others and see who can inflict the most damage to earn weekly rewards.

  • Take part in large-scale conflicts with players around the world
